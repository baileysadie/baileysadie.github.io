5 Cool Facts About Uruguay

  1. The capital city is Montevideo.
  2. Uruguay was the first country to legalize marijuana.
    • Government-regulated sales
    • Private home cultivation
  3. It has more cows than people!
  4. The national dish is called "Chivito" (steak sandwich).
  5. Uruguay has the longest national anthem in the world.

Language

The official language is Spanish, but you'll also hear Portuñol near the Brazil border.

Flag

Music

Candombe is a traditional style of music and dance with African roots, unique to Uruguay.
